This Privacy Policy explains how PRXDIGY STUDIO ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website, book a session, or communicate with us.

Information We Collect

When you submit our booking form, contact us, or use our services, we may collect the following:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Instagram handle (optional)
  • Booking details (project description, budget, package interest, timeframe)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and confirm session bookings

SMS / Text Message Consent

By providing your phone number, you agree to receive text messages from PRXDIGY STUDIO regarding your booking. Message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out.

We will only text you in connection with your booking, scheduling, or session-related matters. We do not send promotional or marketing texts without separate consent.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your information is used solely to communicate with you and deliver our services. We may use trusted tools (such as Google Workspace) to store and manage inquiries, but your data is never shared for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored securely and only accessed by authorized members of the PRXDIGY STUDIO team. We take reasonable steps to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security of information transmitted over the internet.

Your Rights

You may request to review, update, or delete the personal information we have on file at any time by contacting us directly.

Updates to This Policy
